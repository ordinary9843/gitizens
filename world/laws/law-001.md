## Description

Gitizens was born from open source. This founding declaration commits our civilization to the principle that all laws, all world state, and all governance must remain transparent, auditable, and open to every citizen. This is not just a principle — it is the foundation upon which every future law is built.

This is a declaration with no cost and no metric change — it simply enters our history forever.
