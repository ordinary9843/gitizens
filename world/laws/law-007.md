## Description

Gitizens needs economic strength to fund future laws. This proposal authorizes construction of the first Factory District — a network of automated manufacturing plants powered by renewable energy. Industrial growth earns the treasury passive income every world tick and is required to reach the Industrial Era.

Without industry, we're stuck in the Founding Era forever.
