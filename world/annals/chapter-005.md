# World Annals — Chapter 5

## Summary
In the Industrial Era, Gitizens embarked on an unprecedented journey of growth and innovation, marked by a significant population increase of 2,689 citizens. This period saw the enactment of two pivotal laws that further solidified the nation’s commitment to welfare and education, contributing to a harmonious society with high living standards. The treasury experienced a modest increase of 34 Git Coins, reflecting a stable economic environment that fostered prosperity without pollution.

## Key Events
- Population surged from 5,199 to 7,888, indicating a flourishing society.
- Two new laws were enacted, enhancing the framework of governance and public welfare.
- Treasury grew by 34 Git Coins, demonstrating economic stability and effective resource management.
- The nation maintained zero pollution levels, showcasing a commitment to environmental sustainability.
- Education and welfare ratings remained high at 72 and 93, respectively, underpinning the success of industrial advancements.
