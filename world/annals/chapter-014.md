# World Annals — Chapter 14

## Summary
In ticks 131 to 140 of the Modern Era, Gitizens experienced a significant population surge, with an increase of 101,660 individuals bringing the total to 341,754. This demographic growth was accompanied by legislative advancements, with two new laws enacted to further support the flourishing society. The treasury also saw a modest rise, reflecting the prosperity of the era, allowing for continued investments in education, industry, and welfare, all achieving perfect scores of 100.
