# World Annals — Chapter 16

## Summary
In the Modern Era, the Gitizens civilization continued to flourish, marked by significant population growth and robust economic stability. With the enactment of two new laws during ticks 151 to 160, the governance structure adapted to meet the evolving needs of its citizens, ensuring a harmonious balance between progress and tradition. The society celebrated its achievements in education, industry, welfare, green policy, and defense, all while maintaining an impressive record of zero pollution and high stability.

## Key Events
- **Population Surge**: The population increased by 166,800, reaching a total of 699,024 citizens.
- **Economic Growth**: The treasury saw an increase of 17,788 Git Coins, bolstering the nation's financial health.
- **Legislative Milestones**: Two new laws were enacted, bringing the total to 41, enhancing the governance framework.
- **Environmental Success**: The civilization maintained a pollution level of zero, showcasing its commitment to green policies.
- **Stability and Prosperity**: Despite rapid growth, the stability rating held steady at 60, indicating effective management of resources and citizen welfare.
