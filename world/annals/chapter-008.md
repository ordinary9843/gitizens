# World Annals — Chapter 8

## Summary
In the transition from ticks 71 to 80 of the Modern Era, Gitizens experienced significant growth and stability, solidifying its reputation as a utopia of welfare and education. The population surged by 7,410, reflecting the thriving conditions of the nation, while the treasury saw a modest increase of 245 Git Coins. During this period, two new laws were enacted, further enhancing the societal framework and contributing to the overall well-being of the citizens.

## Key Events
- Population increased by 7,410, reaching a total of 24,000 residents.
- Treasury grew by 245 Git Coins, bringing the total to 809 Git Coins.
- Enactment of two new laws, enhancing the stability and governance of Gitizens.
- Continued excellence in welfare, education, and industry, with ratings of 100, 84, and 92, respectively.
- Maintained zero pollution levels, further solidifying Gitizens’ commitment to a sustainable future.
