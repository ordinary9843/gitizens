# World Annals — Chapter 2

## Summary
In the Founding Era of Gitizens, the burgeoning society witnessed a surge in population and a commendable commitment to welfare and education. The period encompassing ticks 11 to 20 was marked by the enactment of three pivotal laws, which further solidified the framework of governance. Despite a decrease in the treasury, the overall stability of the community remained intact as the citizens thrived in a pollution-free environment, with rising optimism for the future.

## Key Events
- The population grew by 1,100, reflecting the appeal of the new society.
- Three new laws were enacted, bringing the total count to 12, enhancing social and economic structures.
- The treasury experienced a decline of 219 Git Coins, prompting discussions about fiscal responsibility and sustainability.
- Welfare and education metrics remained strong at 73 and 71, respectively, showcasing the society’s commitment to its citizens.
- The pollution level remained at zero, contributing to the overall health and happiness of the populace.

## Citizen Voices
- "As our community grows, I feel a sense of belonging and hope. The laws being passed truly reflect our values!" - Elara, a young educator.
- "While I appreciate the focus on welfare, I worry about our dwindling treasury. We need to balance generosity with responsibility." - Torin, a local entrepreneur.
