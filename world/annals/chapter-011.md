# World Annals — Chapter 11

## Summary
In the Modern Era, Gitizens has flourished, reaching unprecedented heights in welfare, education, and environmental sustainability. The population swelled to 102,203, marking an increase of 40,280 since the last account, while the treasury saw a modest rise of 2,693 Git Coins. With no pollution to speak of and an unwavering commitment to green policies, the citizens basked in the glow of their nation’s achievements, heralding a golden age of prosperity and social equity.
