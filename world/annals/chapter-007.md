# World Annals — Chapter 7

## Summary
In ticks 61 to 70 of the Modern Era, Gitizens experienced a significant boost in population and a modest increase in their treasury, reflecting the flourishing state of society. The enactment of two new laws during this period further solidified governance and public welfare, ensuring that the citizens enjoyed an exceptional quality of life. As industries thrived and education levels rose, Gitizens reveled in a pollution-free environment, contributing to their overall happiness and stability.

## Key Events
- Population surged by 4,026, bringing the total to 16,299 citizens.
- Treasury increased by 392 Git Coins, reaching a total of 471 Git Coins.
- Two new laws were enacted, enhancing the legal framework of the society.
- Welfare remained at a perfect score of 100, supported by robust industry and educational systems.
- Pollution levels remained at zero, underscoring the commitment to green policies.
