# World Annals — Chapter 10

## Summary
In this pivotal chapter of Gitizens' history, the Modern Era continues to thrive with a remarkable population growth of 22,146, showcasing the society's robust welfare system and vibrant economy. While the treasury faced a slight decline of 1,205 Git Coins, the overall stability remained intact, bolstered by sound industry and educational policies. The enactment of three new laws during these ticks reflects the government’s ongoing commitment to enhancing the quality of life for all citizens.
